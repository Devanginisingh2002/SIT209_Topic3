Summarise Of Activites

Activity 1

This activity is about IoT Apps as IoT applications, What are the main features, and what are some examples of that. I learned about IoT applications it runs on IoT devices and can be created to be specific to almost every industry, smart appliances, including health care, and more.

Mainly, it is an interface connecting hardware along with the provision of various features connected through the means of the internet. Some examples are as follows, Smart homes, wearable devices(watches) connected cars or more.

Activity 2

This activity is about a smart building, what do smart means, scenarios of a smart building, and the main features needed to build smart buildings. During the activity, I completely understand that with the help of the internet what we can do is make smart things.

Smart means the right use of technology to solve complex problems in a simple manner. The main requirement to make things smart is feasible, economically viable, central control on data and resources, energy-efficient, and telecommunications. Also, efficient sensors, actuators, smart lights, smart echo systems are important for smart buildings.

Activity 3

This activity is about Control Interface, in this, I learned what are the high-level requirements, who uses the control interface, what parts are needed most. I study the control interface it refers to the input devices and interfaces, for example, mouse, joystick, keyboard or eye gaze camera or audible (e.g., screen reader software or auditory scanning).

The required feature is an accessible UI, machine learning, remote access, and easily embedded in other systems.
